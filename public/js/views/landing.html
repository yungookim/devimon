Keeping your device safe.

About

You are working on your lovely dovely laptop in a coffee shop, library, school, or any public spaces, and you have to step out for coffee, smoke, washroom, friend, etc. Simply put Devimon on and you are all set to go!

Sign Up
How it works

When you bring Devimon up on your browser, the browser maintains a conversation with Devimon. As soon as your browser stops talking (when the laptop is closed), Devimon assumes that somebody has closed the laptop, possibly with the intention of stealing it, and sends you an immediate SMS message.
